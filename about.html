History

The Reszka Family founded Jiffy Treet in 1948 and we have been serving Ellettsville for over 70 years. There's more history information which I can get from my boss later.

Jiffy Treet Today

Jiffy Treet is located just off of Highway 46. We offer outdoor seating and are open year-round.

All of our over 50 homemade flavors are made from scratch in the back of the store. Our soft-serve flavors include (those marked with * change periodically):

  • Fruit Swirl*
  • Flavor Burst
  • 2 Dream De Lite (Sugar-Free) Flavors*

  • Non-Fat French Vanilla Yogurt

Food

We offer everything from fried food favorites like french fries, cheese sticks and fried pickles to sandwiches and salads that are made fresh to order.
