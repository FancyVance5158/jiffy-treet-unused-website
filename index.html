Welcome to Jiffy Treet

Located right off of Highway 46, Jiffy Treet is the perfect place for a fresh meal, eating ice cream with friends, or just for a quick snack!

  • Homemade ice cream made in-store
  • Delicious soft-serve with sugar-free and non-dairy options (ask for our current flavors!)
  • Salads and Sandwiches made with the freshest ingredients
  • Made-to-order fried food
  • Outdoor seating and plenty of space to park
  • Open all year round
